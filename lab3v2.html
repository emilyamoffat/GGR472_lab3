This interactive map helps you see how far the Bruce Trail is from UofT campus! Click on the map, and a line navigating from UofT to the point will appear, calculating the time it will take to drive there with traffic.

The Bruce Trail is publicly accessible, running along the Niagara Escarpment from Niagara to Tobermory - through the GTA. Follow the link below for more details.
